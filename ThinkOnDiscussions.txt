🚀 Phase 1 (Core Discussion Features - Must Have)
Post Creation – Users can create discussions with text, images, and documents.
Comments & Replies – Allow threaded discussions.
Categories & Threads – Organize discussions into different topics.
Tagging & Mentions (@user, @club) – Notify relevant people.
Upvotes/Downvotes – Helps prioritize valuable discussions.
Pinned Posts – Club admins can highlight important discussions.
Report & Flag System – Users can report inappropriate content.



🌟 Phase 2 (Engagement & Interaction - Nice to Have)
Polls & Surveys – Users can create polls for opinions.
Notifications & Alerts – Notify users about new posts in followed topics.
Trending Topics Section – Showcase most active discussions.
Anonymous Posting Option – For sensitive discussions.
Role-Based Access – Club admins moderate discussions.


🔥 Phase 3 (Advanced Features - Future Enhancements)
AI-Based Personalized Recommendations – Suggest relevant discussions.
Real-Time Chat Integration – Convert discussions into instant chats.
AI-Driven Topic Summarization – Generate summaries for long discussions.
