📂 Core Resource Categories (Essential)
Event Resources – Presentations, event recordings, brochures, and schedules.
Study Materials – Notes, research papers, coding tutorials, problem sets.
Club Documents – Guidelines, meeting minutes, club constitutions.
Forms & Templates – Registration forms, feedback forms, proposal templates.
Recorded Sessions – Webinars, guest lectures, training sessions.
Internship & Career Resources – Resume guides, interview tips, industry reports.
📁 Additional Enhancements (For Better Experience)
Categorized Storage – Organize resources by topic, club, event, or user role.
Search & Filters – Let users filter by type (PDF, video, link, document).
Download & Preview – Allow previewing before downloading.
Resource Requests – Users can request missing materials.
🚀 Advanced Features (Future Scope)
AI-Based Recommendations – Suggest related resources based on user activity.
Version Control – Track updates on frequently modified documents.
Collaboration Tools – Allow multiple users to upload, edit, and discuss resources.
Restricted Access Controls – Define who can view/download specific resources.
